Rule

Rules of Ice-break Bingo

1 ．Do a self-introduction with your group members. Everyone in the group should act together throughout the game.

2 ．Boxes can be filled by talking to other groups and writing the name of the person who matches the description in the box. The number of the box is linked to the number of the description.

※Please choose your role within your team: two people to keep the question page open and one to fill in the Bingo card.

※Please screenshot your player code after requesting a Bingo card.(Click here to check how to take a screenshot on your iPhone.)

3 ．You may fill up to two boxes per single group. You can also ask the game facilitators, but only one box per person can be filled. (Those wearing the party hat are the game facilitators.)

4 ．You need one row of Bingo to win and get a prize.

※Please come to the prize desk with all the members of your team.

5 ．The game ends once the top 24 teams’ bingo. (16 teams will get a luxury prize.)

6 ．If two teams Bingo at the same time, rock-paper-scissors to choose who comes first.

7 ．Finally, cheating is strictly prohibited! Please also be sure to take care of your own prize.



[Important] Regarding Wildcards (mission)

・The box in the middle of the Bingo card may seem like a free box, but it is not open. To check this box, you will need to succeed in a wildcard-mission.

・A wild card is an optional mini game to connect with your teammates.

・To take part in a wild card mission, approach a person wearing a party hat.

・If you succeed in achieving the mission, you will be given a card that confirms the center box is checked off. Please show it when receiving your prize.

Wildcard choices

1 ．Post photos of you and your teammates wearing NetApp goods on social media such as Instagram LinkedIn Facebook. (Please come to the photobooth for this mission.)

2 ．Connect with your team on social media.

3 ．Shiritori → This is a Japanese word game that requires you to connect the last letter of the word to the next. Mission is achieved once you reach NetApp. Eg: Cat→Tokyo→ONTAP→Place→E-series …etc

4 ．Self-introduction Q&A

※Each member will come up with one question regarding their self-introduction. If your team answers correctly, the mission is complete. (For instance, for a team of 3, 1 question per person will be a total of 3 questions.)
